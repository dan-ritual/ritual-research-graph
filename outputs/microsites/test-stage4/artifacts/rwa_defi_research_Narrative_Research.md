# RWA DeFi Research - Narrative Research Notes

## 1. Current Market Narratives

### Key Themes

**Institutional Adoption Through Tokenization**: The dominant narrative centers on traditional financial institutions like BlackRock scaling tokenized real-world assets (RWA) including real estate, commodities, and corporate debt for DeFi integration. This represents a fundamental shift from speculative crypto assets toward compliant, institutional-grade offerings.

**"On-Chain Finance" (OnFi) Evolution**: The market is transitioning from traditional DeFi to OnFi, characterized by tokenized assets, institutional credit pools, cross-chain interoperability, and AI-powered risk management. RWA and stablecoins are positioned as central to this evolution.

**Regulatory Clarity as Growth Catalyst**: The U.S. GENIUS Act and EU MiCA regulations are driving 2026 growth by providing stablecoin oversight and compliance frameworks, enabling institutional RWA/DeFi adoption while curbing speculation.

### Areas of Consensus
- RWA represents the next major DeFi growth vector
- Regulatory compliance is essential for institutional adoption
- Tokenization of traditional assets will democratize access
- Stablecoins serve as critical infrastructure for RWA-DeFi integration

### Emerging Trends
- **Portfolio Margining Across Asset Classes**: Integration of crypto, equities, and other RWAs in unified margin systems
- **Self-Custody Trading Infrastructure**: Moving away from traditional intermediaries like DTCC
- **Cross-Chain RWA Interoperability**: Assets moving seamlessly across different blockchain networks
- **AI-Powered Risk Management**: Advanced algorithms managing credit and market risks in tokenized asset pools
